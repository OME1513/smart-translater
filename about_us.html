Hi, I'm Om Tiwari!

I am a passionate Class 10th D student who loves technology and languages. I developed Smart Translator to break language barriers and make communication more accessible for everyone. Through this project, I aim to simplify translation, help others connect, and inspire innovation.

Project Journey Timeline

Late December 2024

Research & Planning: Explored various translation APIs, drafted initial wireframes, and set the foundation for a user-friendly interface.

Early January 2025

Development Phase: Started building the translator using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Integrated design elements such as flag icons and dark mode.

Mid-January 2025

Integration & Testing: Added interactive features like speech-to-text and text-to-speech. Tested extensively and refined the user interface based on feedback.

Third Week of January 2025

Finalization & Launch: Polished the final version, incorporated innovative features, and launched Smart Translator alongside this About Us page.
